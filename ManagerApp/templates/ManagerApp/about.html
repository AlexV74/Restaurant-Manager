About Us

Welcome to our family-run Mexican restaurant! Our story began several years ago when our family decided to bring our passion for authentic Mexican cuisine to the local community. We come from a long line of skilled cooks and have spent countless hours perfecting our recipes to create the delicious and flavorful dishes that you'll find on our menu.

Location

Our homey Mexican restaurant is conveniently located near Royal Holloway University, just a short walk away from campus. We are proud to be a part of the vibrant community of Egham, Surrey, and we welcome students, faculty, and staff from Royal Holloway, as well as local residents and visitors.

We believe that our location is one of the things that makes our restaurant special. We are situated in a beautiful and historic part of Egham, surrounded by lush greenery and picturesque landscapes. Whether you're looking for a quick bite to eat between classes or a relaxing dinner with friends, our location provides the perfect setting for a memorable dining experience.

Meet the Team

Juan - Head Chef

Juan is the heart and soul of our kitchen. He has over 20 years of experience in the restaurant industry and has mastered the art of creating authentic Mexican dishes. Juan is passionate about using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and takes pride in every dish he creates.

Maria - Front of House Manager

Maria is the friendly face that greets you when you walk through our doors. She has a talent for making every customer feel like a part of our family. Maria is also responsible for managing our front of house operations, ensuring that your dining experience is nothing but exceptional.

Carlos - Bar Manager

Carlos is the mastermind behind our delicious margaritas and cocktails. He has a passion for mixology and takes pride in creating unique and flavorful drinks that perfectly complement our menu.
